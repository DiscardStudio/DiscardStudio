Discard Studio

Misfits Making Games

Discard Studio is a Small Group of Friends who finally decided "Let's Make a Video Game" shouldn't just be a turn of phrase.

>Millions of people currently in their 20s right now have had the thought. Hundreds of Thousands have the skills.

How many actually decide to put themselves out there and try?


For the longest time, We were among the many. Among those who decided it wasn't worth trying.


Among those who told us not to try.


I'm used to people playing the Devil's Advocate. Frankly, I got pretty sick of it myself.


I've decided to do what I love, for the Love of the Game. And for that, I've been called Unrealistic.


Boo Fucking Who.

Yeah, we'll be fine.

I don't want to hear any more "Devil's Advocates". Look me in the eyes and be inspired or get the fuck out of the way.
